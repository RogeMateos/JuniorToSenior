1Ô∏è‚É£ What is Really "The Cloud"?

Cloud = rent infrastructure on demand, pay for use, and automate everything.

Before:

  • You bought physical servers
  • Installed them in an office
  • Paid everything upfront (CAPEX)
  • Scaling was slow and expensive

Now:

  • You rent servers in minutes
  • Scale automatically
  • Pay only what you use (OPEX)
